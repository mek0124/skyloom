About Skyloom

What Is Skyloom?

Skyloom is a real-time weather reporting application that operates based off either your location or the address you put in when you registered/last-edited your account.

How Is My Data Secure?

Data that is stored in the database that the program will need to read again, like your address, email, phone, etc. is encrypted . Data that is stored in the database that the program does not need to read again, like your password, is hashed .
